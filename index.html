What is the mole fraction?
The mole fraction is the unit of the amount of a constituent (ni) divided by the total amount of all constituents in a mixture (n tot). It is also called the molar fraction.
How to calculate mole fraction?
To calculate mole fraction you should use the following formula:
xi = ni / ntot
where:
xi = mole fraction
ni = amount of a constituent
ntot = total amount of all constituents in a mixture
Why use mole fraction?
The mole fraction is used to represent the number of molecules of a specific component in a mixture devided by the total number of moles in the mixture. In smiple words the mole fraction is used to express hte concentration of a solution.
